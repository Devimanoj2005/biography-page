Tovino Thomas

"The Charming Star of Malayalam Cinema"

About

Tovino Thomas is an Indian actor and film producer who works primarily in Malayalam cinema. Known for his versatility and charisma, Tovino has captured the hearts of millions with his performances in both commercial hits and critically acclaimed films.

Notable Achievements

  • Starred in blockbuster movies like *Minnal Murali*, *Mayanadhi*, and *Virus*
  • Won Kerala State Film Award for Second Best Actor
  • Became a national sensation with his role as *Minnal Murali*, India’s own superhero
  • Also known for his work in promoting social issues and humanitarian causes
